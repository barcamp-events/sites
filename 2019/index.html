What's changed with BarCamp?

Quite a few things, actually! We're looking to make it funner and more engaging for attendees. We don't have specific tracks anymore - Technology, Design, Entrepenuership, Kitchensink are gone. Now, we just have two tracks. And both are that "Kitchen Sink" kind of thing - anyone can sign up and talk about whatever. We have two tracks because we noticed that the four tracks later in the day would be empty, leading people to leave for the day. We ditched the specific tracks because we want this event to be wildly seredipitous to attendees.

Next, this event is not put on by AIGA Nebraska. In fact, this is a different event all together. Notice the domain is "barcampomaha.org"? We're a working group of contributors who will be trying to make the best parts of BarCamp events available to anyone and everyone. This is also going to mark the next generation of events in the Omaha and Lincoln areas. There's a lot more to come here - stick around and keep in touch!
